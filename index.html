What is Carbodo?

Carbodo is a website that will help you figure out the best solution to reducing the fossil fuel emissions based on where it is located. The solutions might not work for everyone but they are still a great way to start. WE HAVE TO REDUCE OUR CARBON EMISSIONS BY 2050 OR THERE WON’T BE A FUTURE FOR US!

Our Mission

Our Mission is to reduce Carbon Emissions around the globe by 50% in the next 30 years. We want to create solutions to reduce the fossil fuel production around the world by partnering up with other organizations and create a solution together to change the planet’s carbon emissions.
